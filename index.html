FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes you different from other agencies?

We only work with B2B SaaS companies. That's it. This means every framework, every creative angle, every optimization we've learned comes from companies like yours. We also own the entire funnel - ads, landing pages, VSL, email flows - so you're not coordinating between five different vendors.

How long until I see results?

We launch campaigns within 14 days of kickoff. Most clients see their first booked demos within the first 7 days. Meaningful data on what's working usually comes by week 2-3. Full optimization and scaling typically happens over 6-8 weeks.

What's the minimum ad spend required?

We recommend a minimum of $200/day in ad spend. This gives us enough data to test creative angles, optimize campaigns, and scale what's working.

Do you work with PLG or self-serve companies?

We primarily work with sales-led B2B SaaS. If your main GTM motion is 100% product led growth, we're probably not the right fit. If you have a sales team and want to drive more qualified demos, that's where we excel.

What if I've tried paid ads before and they didn't work?

Most B2B SaaS companies fail at paid ads for one of three reasons: wrong offer, wrong creative, or wrong funnel. We fix all three. We've worked with companies who burned through agencies before finding us - the difference is we understand B2B SaaS buying cycles and build everything around booking qualified demos, not vanity metrics.

How involved do I need to be?

During the first 2-3 weeks, we'll need more of your time - sales call recordings, offer feedback, creative approvals. After launch, it's typically a 30-minute call per week plus Slack for communication. We handle the execution.

Do you handle creative production or do I need to film content?

We handle everything - scripting, design, editing. For video ads and VSLs, we'll need you or your team to film the talking head portions. We provide the scripts and shot lists, you just need to spend 1 hour per month (approximately) on filming.

What platforms do you run ads on?

Primarily Meta (Facebook and Instagram). We've seen Meta have the lowest CAC, and highest ROAS across 60+ B2B SaaS clients we've worked with.

How do you charge? What's the pricing model?

We have setup, monthly, 3-month or 6-month plans depending on what you're looking for. Our pricing ranges from $5k - $30k across our plans and offers. We can share specific pricing on a call once we understand your situation.

Do I own everything you create?

Yes. All landing pages, VSL scripts, ad creatives, email flows - everything we build is yours. If we part ways, you keep all assets and can continue running campaigns yourself.

What if it doesn't work?

Our goal is to make you profitable within 90 days. So we guarantee results, and offer a full refund if results are not met. If we've done our job and you've held up your end (ad spend, timely approvals, following our process), you will see results.
